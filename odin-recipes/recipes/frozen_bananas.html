Frozen Banana dessert

Description

Easy, healthy dessert with frozen banana.

Ingredients

  • bunch of bananas
  • Preferred add-on (peanut butter, yogurt, blueberries)

Steps

  1. Dice up a bunch of bananas and freeze them over night
  2. Once thoroughly frozen, put a cup of the frozen, diced bananas in a bowl.
  3. Add on just enough of your desired add-on to flavor. I like to put on a tablespoon of peanut butter.
