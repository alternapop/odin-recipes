Broccoli (pan fried)

Description

Broccoli, pan fried, is delicious and easy. You'll never cook it another way.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli (1 large)
  • Olive Oil, 2 tablespoons
  • Pepper / Salt

Steps

  1. Put a medium/large pan on the stove on medium heat.
  2. Cut the broccoli in smaller bite size portions. Remove the bottom half of the large stem.
  3. Put the broccoli into the heated pan. Add a half teaspoon of pepper and enough salt to taste.
  4. Add in about 2 ounces of hot water to create steam, then put a lid on the pan.
  5. Cook for 3.5-4 minutes, then stir, and cook another 3.5-4 minutes.
  6. If you prefer it more charred then you may need to cook another minute longer or so. You can add another ounce of water to prevent burning and sticking.
  7. Remove from pan and serve.
